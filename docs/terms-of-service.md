denai is an AI-assisted clinical workflow platform. It organizes clinical cases, generates treatment pathway suggestions from clinician-provided inputs, and supports workflow progression from planning through laboratory communication.

**denai is not:**

- **A diagnostic system.** denai does not diagnose conditions. Nothing it generates constitutes a clinical diagnosis.
- **Medical advice.** No output from denai constitutes a clinical recommendation, treatment directive, or clinical consultation in a medical or legal sense.
- **A medical device.** denai is not registered or cleared as a medical device in any jurisdiction.

## 3. AI-generated recommendations

denai's recommendation engine analyzes structured clinical inputs — tooth position, bone level, hygiene status, and related factors you provide — to generate treatment pathway suggestions. These suggestions are produced by a programmatic inference model operating on the inputs entered.

The following applies to all recommendations:

- Recommendations are derived solely from the inputs you provide. They do not incorporate factors outside those inputs, including direct clinical observation, patient history, contraindications, or systemic health conditions.
- Recommendations are expressed as a **recommendation strength**, not as a probability, certainty percentage, or measure of clinical accuracy.
- Recommendations are informational workflow aids. They are not diagnoses, treatment prescriptions, or directives for patient care.
- The recommendation engine runs entirely on your device. No clinical data is transmitted to any external AI service to generate these outputs.

denai does not warrant the accuracy, completeness, or clinical suitability of any recommendation generated. Clinical suitability is evaluated and determined by the treating clinician.
